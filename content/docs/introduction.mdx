Parchment is a **privacy-first, self-hostable** maps and navigation app. You run it on your own server; your data stays with you.

- **Self-hosted** — No account required from a third party; deploy on your hardware or VPS.
- **Open** — Built on open data and open source; [AGPL-3.0](https://github.com/alexwohlbruck/parchment/blob/main/LICENSE) licensed.

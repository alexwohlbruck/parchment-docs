title: Transitland

---

Transitland is an open data platform aggregating public transit schedules from agencies worldwide. The integration adds a transit layer to the map and surfaces stop departure information on place cards.

---

## Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Map Layer** | Displays transit routes and stops as a map overlay |
| **Transit Data** | Provides departure times and route information for transit stops |
| **Place Info** | Fetches stop detail by Transitland stop ID |

---

## Configuration

In **Settings → Integrations**, click the settings icon on the Transitland card.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **API Key** | Your Transitland API key. Create one at [transit.land/documentation](https://www.transit.land/documentation). |

Click **Test Connection** before saving.

---

## Notes

Transit data availability depends on agency coverage in your region. Transitland aggregates GTFS feeds, so coverage is best in North America, Europe, and Australia. See [transit.land/feeds](https://www.transit.land/feeds) to check coverage for your area.

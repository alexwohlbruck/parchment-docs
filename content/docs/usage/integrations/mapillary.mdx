---
title: Mapillary
description: Street-level imagery and Street View via Mapillary.
---

Mapillary is a crowd-sourced street-level imagery platform. The integration adds a Mapillary overlay layer to the map and powers the Street View feature.

---

## Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Map Layer** | Displays Mapillary coverage as an overlay on the main map |
| **Street View** | Shows street-level imagery for a location |

---

## Configuration

In **Settings → Integrations**, click the settings icon on the Mapillary card.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **API Key** | Your Mapillary client access token. Create one at [mapillary.com/dashboard/developers](https://www.mapillary.com/dashboard/developers). |

Click **Test Connection** before saving.

---

## Notes

Mapillary imagery coverage varies by location. Areas with no coverage will show a fallback state in the Street View panel. The Mapillary API is free for non-commercial use.
